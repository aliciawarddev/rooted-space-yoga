About

Our Space

Rooted Space is intentionally small. We believe transformation happens in intimate settings where teachers know your name and your practice. Our studio holds just 12 students, ensuring personalized attention and genuine connection.

Located in the heart of Calgary, our light-filled studio features natural wood floors, floor-to-ceiling windows, and all the props you need. We provide mats, blocks, bolsters, and blankets—just bring yourself.

Teachers

Sarah Chen

Lead Teacher - Vinyasa & Restorative

Sarah brings 8 years of teaching experience and a belief that yoga should feel accessible, not intimidating. Her classes blend mindful movement with practical philosophy you can take off the mat.

Maya Patel

Teacher - Restorative & Meditation

Maya's teaching focuses on the nervous system and deep rest. After years in corporate law, she discovered yoga as a path back to herself—and now guides others to do the same.

David Kim

Teacher - Meditation & Breathwork

David teaches meditation without the mysticism. His sessions are grounded, practical, and designed for skeptical minds that need proof that stillness works.
